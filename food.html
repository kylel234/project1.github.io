Some favorites of mine

  • Ramen

    • A delicious japanese noodle soup. Usually topped with green onions, miso broth, chashu pork, dried seaweed, and a naruto fish cake

  • Taiwanese Minced Pork on Rice
    • Reccomend one from Pine and Crane

    A delicious and simple rice dish. Usually made with minced pork, egg, soy sauce, and green onions

  • Beef Noodle Soup

    • A delicious and simple noodle soup. Usually topped with sliced beef, coriander, green onions and bok choy
